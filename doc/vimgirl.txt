*vimgirl.txt*  For Vim version 7.0 or later.                         *vimgirl*

Vimgirl - a dead simple filetype for nested lists~

In files that have the "vimgirl" 'filetype' set, you can fold and unfold
subtrees with <Space>. Folded trees are blue. That's all.

You can also use all available commands related to folding, see |fold.txt|.

 vim:tw=78:et:ft=help:norl:
